# About-me
Details Repository about Me

Maica Group is a leading WPC Door and Frame manufacturer in India. Maica Group supplies a wide range of products all over India, including WPC Doors, WPC Door Frames, Solid PVC sheets, and Charcoal Wall Louver Panels. With a commitment to excellence and innovation, Maica supplies the best products, ensuring durability, resistance to environmental factors, and an appealing aesthetic. If you are looking for a trusted supplier of WPC Doors, Solid PVC Sheets, or wall louvers, Maica Group welcomes you to partner with us.

At Maica Plastiwood Private Limited, we manufacture high-quality WPC Doors and WPC Frames, and supply high-quality PVC sheets and charcoal wall louvers all over India. Our WPC Door & Frame manufacturing Plants are located in Indore, MP. Maica Plastiwood was founded with the aim of providing durable and eco-friendly products, delivering a change towards a better India. WPC Doors and frames are the future of traditional furniture, and we focus on sustainably manufacturing them. Maica Plastiwood is expanding rapidly, and we are actively partnering with wholesalers, retailers, and dealers to supply our products all over India.
